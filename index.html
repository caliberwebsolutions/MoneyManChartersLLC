Our Story

The Money Man Difference

Money Man Charters was built on a simple idea — put people on fish and give them an experience they'll never forget. Based out of Destin, FL, we run a tight, professional operation with a passion for the sport and the sea.

Whether you're a seasoned angler looking for trophy fish or a family wanting a fun day on the water, we tailor every trip to your group. Our captain knows these waters inside and out — from the nearshore reefs to the deep offshore drops.

We're fully licensed, insured, and Coast Guard certified. Your safety and satisfaction are always our top priority.
